Problem Statement:
There is a need for international students with little to no experience to gain more opportunities in order to get better job employment chances and overall success.

Why I Built This:
As an international student, I personally feel the struggle. Gaining relevant experience is challenging when visa restrictions and lack of networks block many paths. During my time working with multiple nonprofits, I saw the other side too‚Äîhow many of them operate with minimal resources and often lack the modern tools needed to engage effectively. Many leaders are not tech-savvy, and that creates a gap when trying to connect with younger audiences. We want to simplify the way both students and nonprofits can collaborate, upskill, and thrive together.

So, what if we widen the gap of opportunities?
