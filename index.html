My Career So Far

I’m currently studying Computer Science and Economics at Boston University, where I explore how technology can solve real-world problems. My journey so far has led me to product roles where I blend strategy, design, and Agile thinking to create purposeful tools.

As a Technical Product Manager at Hack4Impact, I led a student team of UI/UX Designers and SWEs developing full-stack platforms for nonprofits - the latest was an internal Event Management System Platform. With the BU School of Public Health, I spearhead as a Full-Stack Dev / Research Assistant working on a data-driven web application to support tuberculosis screening initiatives and mobile tracking of Peru's Ministry of Health & Socio En Salud.

On the side, I launched Cause Connect, a platform that connects international students with nonprofit projects—offering career-building experiences, whilst helping NPO /NGOs solve pressing hiring challenges.

It’s about creating value on both sides. I’m driven by curiosity, collaboration, and a desire to ideate, build, and bring ideas into reality.
